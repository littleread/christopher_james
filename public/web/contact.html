CONTACT

Spoken Word/Hip-Hop Artist from South Florida. Writing has been a creative outlet since a child. 2pac, Lupe Fiasco, and Eminem were of my earliest and biggest influences. Lyrics and the quality in them were always what I cared about most. I'd skip right past lil Boosie just to get to my classic Pac. Some say my writing is sad and dark.

Since a young age I wrote to solve my pain. To understand what was going through my head. So writing with pain is all I've ever known. And it's what keeps me sane. Now I write to help myself but also others. I speak with passion because I truly care. I rap with a message cause I respect the art. Everything I do has purpose. So I treat it as such. ​ "Work like an athlete, move like an artist"
